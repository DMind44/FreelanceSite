## About

Shadow Keep is a dark fantasy action RPG that combines procedural dungeon generation with strategic turn-based combat. Players explore ever-changing dungeons filled with dangerous enemies and powerful loot.

## What I Did

- Designed and implemented the procedural dungeon generation system
- Built the turn-based combat engine with status effects and combo mechanics
- Created all pixel art sprites and animations using Aseprite
- Implemented the inventory and equipment system

## Technical Highlights

The procedural generation uses a combination of room templates and corridor algorithms to create interconnected dungeon layouts. Each floor increases in difficulty, introducing new enemy types and environmental hazards.

The combat system supports real-time-with-pause gameplay, allowing players to strategically position characters before executing actions. Status effects stack and interact, creating emergent gameplay opportunities.
